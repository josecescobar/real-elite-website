## Why this matters

The Real Elite × A+ Paving "Full Property Perimeter" bundle is live at **realelitecontracting.com/full-property-perimeter**. The bundle landing page is built and indexed on Real Elite's domain (higher SEO authority). The bottleneck to converting tri-state homeowner inbound from that page into closed bundle sales is the A+ side of the experience — specifically, four gaps in A+'s digital footprint.

Closing those gaps is a one-time investment. Real Elite is prepared to co-fund up to 50% of the $3K–$5K project cost as part of the partnership investment.

## Scope — what we're proposing

### Phase 1 — Foundation rebuild (Weeks 1–4) · $1,500–$2,500

**Website:**
- Rebuild apluspavingtoday.com on a modern platform (Next.js or WordPress — your call; we recommend Next.js to match Real Elite's stack for joint marketing)
- HTTPS by default, mobile-first, sub-3-second load, SEO meta and Open Graph tags
- New service pages: Asphalt Driveway Paving · Sealcoating · Parking Lot Paving · Landscaping · Hardscape · Snow Removal (if seasonal)
- Service-area pages: Eastern Panhandle WV · Frederick County MD · Loudoun County VA · Berkeley County · Jefferson County
- Reviews carousel pulling from Google reviews API
- Single contact form routed to A+ owner SMS + email
- Footer + service-area schema markup for local SEO

**Google Business Profile:**
- Photo cadence: 2 fresh job photos per week (we can train a crew member or use CompanyCam)
- Service category cleanup
- Q&A section populated
- "Open" hours updated and accurate
- Post cadence: 1 weekly storm-season post during the April–October paving window

### Phase 2 — Review velocity (Weeks 3–10) · $75/month tooling

- Adopt **NiceJob** ($75/month, no contract) — same platform Real Elite uses
- Auto-triggers SMS review request 24h after job completion
- **Target: +50 verified Google reviews in 6 months** (closes the gap to Slonaker's)
- No incentives offered (FTC compliance + Google's review-incentive ban)

### Phase 3 — Social presence (Weeks 4–8) · $500–$1,000

- Facebook business page (claimed and verified)
- Instagram business profile
- Drone before/after Reels — 2 per week minimum during paving season
- Joint storm-response posts with Real Elite when applicable
- Co-published "Full Property Perimeter" content under both brands

### Phase 4 — BBB accreditation (Weeks 6–10) · $0–$500 (BBB fee varies by revenue tier)

- Apply for BBB accreditation
- Trust signal for higher-income homeowners (Frederick MD, Loudoun VA target markets where BBB still moves the needle)

### Phase 5 — Joint marketing kickoff (Week 10+) · ~$75–$150/month tooling

- Co-branded Meta lead-form ads targeting "Full Property Perimeter" buyer
- Shared CRM pipeline (Real Elite handles intake, splits scope to A+ on bundles)
- Mutual 8% referral on co-sold bundle revenue (capped $3K per project — see master synthesis §5)
- Quarterly review meeting to refine targeting
