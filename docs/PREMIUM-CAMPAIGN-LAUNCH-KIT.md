“Based on what you shared, the right next step is an in-home scope walkthrough. We’ll document existing conditions, priorities, and constraints so the written proposal is based on the actual project. The available windows are [option 1] and [option 2].”

### Respectful no-fit response

“Thank you for walking through the project with us. Based on [scope/location/timing], we are not the best fit for this particular job, and I do not want to waste your time with an appointment. [If appropriate: The type of contractor I would look for is _____.]”

## Qualification rubric

Score each lead consistently. A high budget does not override a poor location, timeline, or service fit.

| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Location | Outside practical service area | Edge market | Loudoun, Jefferson, or Berkeley focus area |
| Service | Repair/small-job mismatch | Adjacent substantial project | Campaign offer match |
| Planning band | Clearly below viable scope | Uncertain/open | Aligned with public band |
| Timeline | No intent/indefinite | 6–12+ months | 0–6 months |
| Decision readiness | Unreachable/no property control | Early research | Homeowner/decision-maker ready to discuss |

**Qualified consultation:** 7–10 points, with no zero in location or service.  
**Nurture:** 4–6 points.  
**No fit:** 0–3 points or a zero in location/service.

## Weekly scorecard

Review every Monday for the prior Monday–Sunday period.

| Metric | Target | Decision use |
|---|---:|---|
| Campaign landing sessions | Baseline first | Denominator only; not success alone |
| Consultation starts | Baseline first | Detect message/form friction |
| Consultation submissions | Track | Raw demand |
| Qualified consultations | 1+ per week after ramp | Primary campaign KPI |
| Four-business-hour contact | 90%+ | Operating discipline |
| Qualified-to-walkthrough | 33%+ | Sales/process fit |
| Walkthrough-to-proposal | Establish baseline | Scope discipline |
| Proposal-to-won | Establish baseline | Offer and pricing fit |
| Cost per qualified lead | Only after paid launch | Media efficiency |

At days 30, 60, and 90, decide whether to scale, revise, or stop each channel independently.
