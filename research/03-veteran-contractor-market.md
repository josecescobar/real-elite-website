## 1. Market Size and Growth (2025-2026)

### Core Statistics
- **1.6 million** veteran-owned businesses in the U.S., generating **~$1.0 trillion** in receipts (2023 ABS data, released Nov 2025). Veterans = 4.3% of all business owners. [SBA Office of Advocacy, 2025](https://advocacy.sba.gov/2025/11/11/facts-about-small-business-veteran-ownership-statistics-2025/)
- **261,000** veteran-owned **employer firms** (4.4% of 5.9M total). [Census Bureau, Nov 2024](https://www.census.gov/library/stories/2024/11/veteran-owned-businesses.html)
- **1.4 million** veteran nonemployer firms generating **$65.7B** in receipts.
- Veteran-owned employer firms generated **5.3% of total employer firm revenue** — disproportionate to their 4.4% headcount share. [Census, 2024](https://www.census.gov/library/stories/2024/11/veteran-owned-businesses.html)
- **Construction is a top-3 sector** for veteran ownership: **~15,800 veteran-owned construction employer firms**. [SBA Advocacy, 2025](https://advocacy.sba.gov/2025/11/11/facts-about-small-business-veteran-ownership-statistics-2025/)

### West Virginia Position
- **West Virginia ranks among the top states** for veteran-owned business density: **7.9% of all firms, 10.8% of employer firms** — nearly 2x the national average. [WV SBA](https://www.westvirginiasba.us/veteran-owned-business-certification.php)

### Construction Market Context
- Total U.S. construction market: **$2.2T in 2025**, projected **$2.31T in 2026** (4.9% CAGR through 2034). [Construction Coverage / Research and Markets, 2025](https://constructioncoverage.com/data/us-construction-spending)
- **Roofing leads exterior renovation activity in 2025** — 22% of homeowners did roofing; 20% windows/skylights; 19% painting/gutters. [Roofing Contractor, 2025](https://www.roofingcontractor.com/articles/102130-roofing-lead-exterior-renovation-activity-in-2025)
- **TPO membrane** is fastest-growing roofing material (6.79% CAGR through 2031); metal roofing has gone mainstream. [Finturf Roofing Trends 2025](https://finturf.com/blog/roofing-industry/)
- **Insurance restoration / storm repair** is a top growth niche — consistent high-volume work driven by claims (relevant to FEMA disaster contracting, see §3).

**Strategic note:** Real Elite competes inside a ~$1T veteran-owned segment, but the federal SDVOSB carve-out is where pricing power lives (next section).

## 2. Certifications and Designations

### 2a. SDVOSB (Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business) — **HIGHEST PRIORITY**
- **Federal set-aside goal raised from 3% to 5%** by the FY2024 NDAA (Section 863) — a 67% increase, pushing the annual target above **$31B**. [Congress.gov CRS IN12313](https://www.congress.gov/crs-product/IN12313)
- **FY2025 SDVOSB awards: $28.6B across ~52,000 contract actions** — gov hit 4.7%, missing the 5% goal by ~$2B (first miss since 2011). [Fed-Spend, 2026](https://fed-spend.com/blog/sdvosb-contract-opportunities-veteran-owned-2026)
- **Self-certification ended Oct 1, 2024.** SBA-issued certification is now mandatory at both prime and subcontract level. [NASBP](https://www.nasbp.org/post/sba-issues-direct-final-rule-eliminating-self-certification-for-service-disabled-veteran-owned-small-businesses/)
- **Process:** Apply via [veterans.certify.sba.gov](https://veterans.certify.sba.gov/). Requires 51%+ ownership/control by a VA- or DoD-rated service-disabled veteran. **No fee.** Active SAM.gov registration required.
- **Processing time:** SBA cleared backlog late 2025 — now **~12 days** (down from 60-90 days). [SBA Press Release, Nov 2025](https://www.sba.gov/article/2025/11/11/sba-clears-vetcert-program-backlog-put-veteran-entrepreneurs-first)
- **Validity:** 3 years; renewal launched in MySBA Certifications Nov 1, 2025.

### 2b. VOSB (Veteran-Owned Small Business) via VetCert
- Same VetCert portal; same 3-year cycle; 51%+ veteran ownership (no disability rating required).
- VetCert **replaced the VA's CVE on Jan 1, 2023** under NDAA 2021. CVE-verified firms got automatic SBA carryover for their remaining eligibility period. [SBA Article, Jan 2023](https://www.sba.gov/article/2023/jan/10/biden-harris-administration-begin-accepting-applications-january-9-sba-veteran-small-business)
- **VOSB matters mainly inside the VA** under Veterans First (see §3); SDVOSB is broader across all agencies.

### 2c. SBA 8(a) Business Development Program
- 9-year program for socially/economically disadvantaged firms. Veterans may qualify but not on veteran status alone. [SBA 8(a)](https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-assistance-programs/8a-business-development-program)
- **As of Oct 1, 2025, 8(a) construction firms must have a Bona Fide Place of Business in the contract performance region** — Martinsburg is well-positioned for WV/MD/VA work.
- Pairs with SDVOSB: agencies with overlapping goals often prefer "stacked" firms.

### 2d. VA "Vets First" Verification
- No longer a separate certification — replaced by VetCert. The VA still operates the **Veterans First Contracting Program** as a procurement preference (see §3a). [VA OSDBU](https://www.va.gov/OSDBU/programs/VeteranSmallBusinessCertificationChanges.asp)

### 2e. HUBZone — **NEEDS ADDRESS LOOKUP**
- Provides 10% price evaluation preference + set-aside competition. Requires principal office in a qualified census tract AND 35% of employees living in a HUBZone.
- Berkeley County, WV contains multiple low-income census tracts (e.g., **9715, 9716** in central Martinsburg) historically eligible. **Action: Verify Real Elite's exact office address at [maps.certify.sba.gov/hubzone/map](https://maps.certify.sba.gov/hubzone/map).** Current map last updated July 2023 from 2020 Census data; next update July 2028. [SBA HUBZone Map](https://maps.certify.sba.gov/hubzone/map/help)

### 2f. State-Level Programs
- **West Virginia:** §5A-3-37 resident-vendor veteran preference — bid penalty up to 5%, requires 75% of employees be 2-year WV residents. Real Elite likely qualifies. [WV Code 5A-3-37](https://code.wvlegislature.gov/5A-3-37/)
- **Maryland VSBE:** State agencies must target **3% of procurement spend** to certified Veteran-owned Small Business Enterprises. **Free certification via eMMA**, 3-year renewal (effective Sept 1, 2025). [Maryland VSBE](https://gomdsmallbiz.maryland.gov/Pages/VSBE-Program.aspx)
- **Virginia SWaM + SDV designation:** Statewide **42% SWaM goal**; service-disabled veterans get an SDV designation in the SWaM database. Requires first obtaining DVS eligibility certificate (804-786-0286), then SBSD SWaM. **Free; ~60 business days to certify.** [Virginia SBSD](https://sbsd.virginia.gov/certification-division/service-disabled-veterans/)

### 2g. GSA Schedule (MAS — formerly 03FAC Facilities)
- Schedule 03FAC consolidated into the Multiple Award Schedule (MAS) "Facilities" category. Provides streamlined procurement for federal facilities maintenance, HVAC, roofing, building maintenance. [GSA 03FAC](https://www.gsa.gov/acquisition/purchasing-programs/gsa-schedules/list-of-gsa-schedules/facilities-maintenance-repair-and-operations/vendor-resources-for-schedule-03fac)
- Stack with SDVOSB → sole-source eligibility on most facility orders.

### 2h. Trade/Manufacturer Certifications
- **GAF Master Elite** — only **~2% of US roofers** hold this. Unlocks Golden Pledge Warranty (50-yr material, 25-yr labor). Requires licensure, insurance, BBB standing. Powerful trust signal pairs naturally with veteran branding. [GAF](https://www.gaf.com/en-us/plan-design/homeowner-education/choose-gaf-roofer)
- **CertainTeed SELECT ShingleMaster** — highest CertainTeed tier; 5-Star warranty (25-yr workmanship + 50-yr material). Includes "Find a Pro" lead generation. [CertainTeed](https://www.certainteed.com/select-shinglemaster)
- **Recommended pairings:** GAF Master Elite + CertainTeed SELECT covers ~70% of US asphalt shingle market. Combined with SDVOSB, Real Elite can claim the rare trifecta: top-tier residential warranty + federal set-aside eligibility + veteran trust.

## 3. Federal & State Contracting Opportunities (WV / MD / VA)

### 3a. VA Construction — Real Elite's Backyard
- **Martinsburg VAMC** sits on **175 acres**, serves **70,000+ veterans** across WV/MD/VA/PA, **2,200+ employees**, 69 acute beds + 178 nursing + 312 domiciliary beds. ~8 miles from Real Elite. Manages 7 community outpatient clinics in **Cumberland, Frederick, Hagerstown MD; Harrisonburg, Winchester VA; Franklin, Petersburg WV** — all inside Real Elite's natural service area. [VA Martinsburg](https://www.va.gov/martinsburg-health-care/about-us/)
- **VA Veterans First Contracting Program (VAAR Subpart 819.70)** — priority order: **(1) SDVOSB → (2) VOSB → (3) 8(a)/HUBZone → (4) other small business**. The VA must consider SDVOSB BEFORE any other set-aside. [VAAR 819.70](https://www.acquisition.gov/vaar/subpart-819.70-va-veterans-first-contracting-program)
- **FY2024 VA awarded $10.2B (23% of all prime contracts) to SDVOSBs** — far above the 5% statutory floor. SDVOSB set-asides alone = $8.4B+. [Fed-Spend](https://fed-spend.com/blog/sdvosb-contract-opportunities-veteran-owned-2026)
- **Sole-source threshold for VA SDVOSB construction: $5M** (vs. $4M FAR-wide). [VAAR 819.7008](https://www.acquisition.gov/vaar/819.7008-sole-source-awards-verified-service-disabled-veteran-owned-small-businesses.)
- **ISI-Markon JV** holds the construction management support contract for VA Maryland HCS infrastructure modernization — a likely prime to subcontract under regionally.

### 3c. SAM.gov Roofing Pipeline
- **NAICS 238160 (Roofing Contractors): $19M small-business size standard.** Real Elite well under this ceiling — full set-aside eligibility. [HigherGov NAICS 238160](https://www.highergov.com/naics/238160-roofing-contractors/)
- **Other key NAICS:** 236220 (Commercial/Institutional Building Construction); 238170 (Siding); 238140 (Masonry); 561730 (Landscaping).
- Daily SDVOSB roofing solicitations on SAM.gov — recent example: American Lake VAMC Building 85 full replacement. [SweetSpot Console](https://console.sweetspotgov.com/federal-contracts/45713b72-d8a0-58a9-8330-8c7c06f50fb8)

### 3d. State Pipelines
- **WV Purchasing Division** — vendor preference plus dedicated veteran portal. [WV Purchasing Veterans](https://www.state.wv.us/admin/purchase/surplus/veterans.html)
- **MD eMMA** — VSBE-coded solicitations, plus Howard County's separate VOBE program. [Howard County VOBE](https://www.howardcountymd.gov/procurement-contract-administration/veteran-owned-business-enterprise-program-vobe)
- **VA eVA** — Cardinal procurement system; SDV-designated firms get visibility in 42% SWaM goal.

### 3e. FEMA Disaster / Storm Restoration
- FEMA uses **Advance Contracts** plus post-event awards; SDVOSB is one of FEMA's recognized set-asides. [FEMA Advance Contracts](https://www.fema.gov/businesses-organizations/doing-business/advanced-contracts)
- Mid-Atlantic derecho events (May 2012, July 2023, July 2025) generated multi-month roofing restoration pipelines across WV/MD/VA. Registering on FEMA's Disaster Response Registry and being **pre-positioned** on regional IDIQs is the entry point.

## 4. Strategic Recommendations for Real Elite

### Tier 1 — Do Immediately (0-90 days)
1. **Apply for SDVOSB via VetCert** if Jose Escobar holds a VA disability rating. With 12-day processing, this is the highest-ROI single action. Unlocks **$28.6B in annual federal demand** with VA priority status sitting 8 miles away.
2. **Verify HUBZone status** of Real Elite's office on [maps.certify.sba.gov/hubzone/map](https://maps.certify.sba.gov/hubzone/map). Martinsburg census tracts 9715/9716 are historically qualified. If eligible, stack HUBZone + SDVOSB → 10% price preference + sole-source up to $5M at VA.
3. **Register on SAM.gov** (free, ~10 business days). Mandatory predicate to every federal opportunity.
4. **Activate WV vendor preference** (§5A-3-37) — file the resident-vendor + veteran certificate with every state bid; 5% effective edge.

### Tier 2 — 90-180 days
5. **Maryland VSBE certification** via eMMA — free, 3% state spend goal, opens Frederick/Hagerstown/Cumberland (all VAMC clinic cities).
6. **Virginia SDV designation** — pursue DVS eligibility cert first, then SWaM. Opens 42% statewide goal + Winchester/Harrisonburg VAMC clinic catchment.
7. **GAF Master Elite application** — pairs with "Military Precision" brand; Golden Pledge becomes the consumer-side trust anchor.
8. **GSA MAS Facilities onramp** — long lead (6-12 months) but converts one-off federal jobs into a 5-year IDIQ vehicle.

### Tier 3 — 180-365 days
9. **Sub to Martinsburg VAMC prime contractors** (ISI-Markon JV; HITT Contracting) for past performance citations before pursuing prime VA roofing awards.
10. **CertainTeed SELECT ShingleMaster** — second manufacturer credential broadens spec-driven commercial work.
11. **FEMA Disaster Response Registry** + position on regional IDIQs for storm-season pipelines.
12. **Pursue Joint Base Andrews / Fort Detrick small-business symposiums** — SDVOSB carries weight in Army/Air Force FACOPS scoring.

### Highest-ROI Single Move
**SDVOSB certification + Martinsburg VAMC targeting.** The VA spent **$10.2B with SDVOSBs in FY2024 (23% of prime spend)**, has a statutory mandate to consider SDVOSBs first, and Real Elite is closer to the medical center than virtually any qualified competitor. Layer in HUBZone (if confirmed) for sole-source eligibility up to $5M per contract.

### Brand Alignment
"Military Precision. Civilian Excellence." maps cleanly onto SDVOSB + GAF Master Elite + Veterans First. The branding is already aligned with the highest-leverage credentials — the work is execution: certify, register, and bid systematically.
