**Prepared for:** Real Elite Contracting LLC (Martinsburg WV) x A+ Paving & Landscaping (Gerrardstown WV)
**Bundle concept:** "Full Property Perimeter" – roof + siding + deck (Real Elite) + asphalt driveway + landscaping (A+ Paving). Tagline: *"From the roof to the road — one team, one standard, Military Precision."*

## 1. Residential Asphalt Paving Profit Margins (Mid-Atlantic, 2025-2026)

### Gross & net margins
- **Top 50 paving contractors (2025):** 40% reported margins above 20%, another 22% in the 16-20% range. This was a ~10 point jump YoY as material prices stabilized. ([Paving Marketers 2025 Market Report](https://pavingmarketers.com/2025-market-report))
- **Industry net margin benchmark:** Paving contractors average ~6.8% net. Smaller paving firms (<$25M revenue) outperform majors on both margin and growth. ([Tupelo SMB - Paving Contractor Valuation](https://www.tupelosmb.com/blog/paving-contractors-private-company-multiples-and-valuation))
- **Top quartile residential pavers** routinely run **25-35% gross margin** on driveways (labor + asphalt + base) and **10-15% net** after fleet, fuel, and insurance. ([Ironline Advisors - Asphalt Paving Value](https://ironlineadvisors.com/industry-focus-creating-value-in-asphalt-paving/))
- Only **26% of paving companies serve residential** — most chase commercial/municipal. Residential is a fragmented, under-served niche. ([Paving Marketers](https://pavingmarketers.com/2025-market-report))

### Cost per sq ft — asphalt vs concrete vs pavers (2025-2026)

| Material | Installed $/sq ft | Lifespan | Maintenance |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Asphalt** | $4-$7 (regional) / $7-$13 (national avg incl. labor) | 15-20 yrs | Reseal every 3-5 yrs at $300-$800 |
| **Concrete** | $8-$20 | 30-40 yrs | Seal every 2-3 yrs ($1-$2/sf) |
| **Pavers** | $10-$30 | 25-30+ yrs | Reseal every 3-5 yrs ($200-$400) |

### Typical project size / total ticket
- **Avg residential driveway: 400-600 sq ft** (single car ~250 sf; 2-car 20'x40' = 800 sf; 3-car ~30-36' wide). ([Joliet Concrete](https://www.jolietconcretesolutions.com/average-size-of-driveway/))
- **National avg install ticket: $4,695** (range $2,925-$6,547). Baltimore avg $4,592. ([Angi](https://www.angi.com/articles/how-much-does-it-cost-install-asphalt-driveway.htm))
- **Eastern Panhandle rural lots often run 800-1,500 sf** (longer driveways) → **typical ticket $4,000-$10,000**.

### Sealcoating margins
- Retail price: **$0.15-$0.25/sf** (low end) up to **$0.80-$1.50/sf** (full national avg). ([HomeAdvisor](https://www.homeadvisor.com/cost/landscape/seal-asphalt-paving/), [AMR NY](https://amrnewyork.com/blog/how-much-is-sealcoating-per-square-foot/))
- Materials: $0.06-$0.38/sf. Labor: ~$0.90-$1.50/sf.
- **Net margin 10-20%** on standalone sealcoat; **gross margin 40-60%** when bundled with a paving job (truck is already on site). Typical 500-600 sf driveway = $225-$600 ticket.

### Seasonality (Mid-Atlantic)
- **Paving season: mid-April through October.** Off-peak Dec 20 – Mar 15. ([Maul Asphalt](https://www.maulasphalt.com/asphalt-sealcoating-considerations-for-the-cold-months/), [Fix Asphalt - When Too Cold to Sealcoat 2025 Mid-Atlantic Guide](https://www.fixasphalt.com/testblog/when-is-it-too-cold-to-sealcoat-asphalt-2025-mid-atlantic-guide-thanksgiving-warm-spell-warning))
- **Sealcoating window:** May 1 – Oct 31 (needs >50°F and rising).
- **Peak demand: June-August.** Fall = budget-flush commercial work; spring = HOA/residential surge.
- **Strategic implication:** Paving's peak (Apr-Oct) is the inverse of roofing's storm season (late summer-winter). Combined calendars give Real Elite + A+ a near-12-month booked schedule.

## 2. Bundled Exterior Renovation Marketing Strategies

### Commission / referral structures that work
- **Passive intro:** 3-7% of contract value. **Active hand-off (joint site visit, co-quote):** 8-15%. ([Get The Referral](https://www.getthereferral.com/blog/referral-fees-best-practices-for-success), [Markup And Profit](https://www.markupandprofit.com/articles/referral-fees-and-sales-commissions/))
- **Industry standard for closed jobs: 10%.** Conservative-but-sustainable: 2-3% with a "not-to-exceed" cap on large jobs.
- **Specialty/sub-contractor referrals: 1.5-3%** (paving falls here for raw lead-shares; bumps to 5-10% when bundled).

**Recommendation:** Real Elite ↔ A+ should run a **mutual 8% on co-sold bundle revenue** (active hand-off model) with a **3% "warm intro only" fallback** when only a phone number is exchanged. Cap at $3,000 per project to protect margin on $40K+ bundles.

### Joint marketing playbooks (proven elsewhere)
- **Roofer + siding bundling:** Single-vendor framing reduces overhead and schedule risk; contractors offering combo discounts close at higher rates. ([QXO](https://www.qxo.com/qxo-blog/build-your-business/how-can-i-market-my-roofing-business-to-offer-siding))
- **Plumbing + HVAC and Landscaper + Roofer** are the two most-cited complementary pairings in trade press; both work because the homeowner is "already in project mode." ([JobNimbus Strategic Partnerships](https://www.jobnimbus.com/blog/strategic-partnerships-for-roofers))
- **Case study – Outsourced Marketing:** Exterior remodeling firm saw **+131% leads, +56% in-home quotes, +608% organic traffic, -50% CPC** by tying SEO + content + integrated marketing across exterior services. ([Outsourced Marketing Case Study](https://www.outsourcedmarketing.ca/home-remodeling-marketing-case-study/))
- **Door-hanger drops on the 3 nearest homes** to every completed job consistently outperform paid ads on CPL. ([Remodel Reach Marketing](https://remodelreachmarketing.com/what-are-the-best-marketing-strategies-for-home-improvement/))

### Co-branded landing pages / lead-share arrangements
- Co-branded pages combine reputations and increase trust; the form on the page captures leads and a backend routes them to the right contractor. ([Floify Co-branding](https://floify.com/blog/cobranding-landing-pages-referral-partners), [Built Construction](https://getbuilt.com/client-resource-center/create-co-branded-landing-page/))
- Best practice: **single hero, two logos, one form, automated round-robin or scope-based routing.** Mobile-first (most home services searches), click-to-call, license/insurance/military-veteran trust signals.

### Bundle pricing psychology: discount vs convenience
- **Bundled prices must show a real discount vs à-la-carte** — buyers detect fake discounts and trust drops. Transparent bundling = **+30% trust, +25% repeat business, 20% market-share lift** vs unbundled competitors. ([WorkWave Insights](https://insights.workwave.com/industry/field-service/price-bundling-tips-for-home-service-businesses/))
- **Contractor research:** A **30% off threshold** is the minimum perceived discount that moves a buyer to try a new offer. ([HIRI - Marketing to Contractors](https://www.hiri.org/blog/market-to-contractors))
- **Convenience framing** ("one crew, one timeline, one warranty") outperforms pure discount language with higher-income exterior buyers — the demographic for full-perimeter bundles.

**Recommendation:** Lead with **convenience + Military Precision warranty** ("one team, one standard"), then anchor with a **5-8% bundle discount** vs the sum of separate quotes. Don't go beyond 10% — it erodes margin and reads as desperation.

## Strategic Recommendations for the Bundle Launch

1. **Build the bundle landing page first**, on Real Elite's domain (better SEO authority than apluspavingtoday.com). URL: `/full-property-perimeter` or `/roof-to-road`. Co-branded hero, two logos, single form, scope-based lead router (roof/siding/deck → Real Elite CRM; driveway/landscape → A+).
2. **Pricing structure:** Sum-of-parts quote with a **transparent 7% bundle discount**, framed as "Military Precision package — one crew, one warranty, one timeline." Avoid >10% discount (margin erosion + perceived desperation).
3. **Referral economics:** Mutual **8% on co-sold revenue** with a **$3,000 cap**; **3% on warm intros** that don't close as bundles. Document in a 1-page mutual referral agreement.
4. **Calendar alignment:** Real Elite leads winter/storm season (Nov-Mar); A+ leads paving season (Apr-Oct). Joint marketing budget weighted 60/40 to whichever partner owns the season.
5. **Fix A+'s digital baseline** as the first joint-marketing investment (~$3-5K):
   - HTTPS + speed/mobile rebuild
   - Google Business Profile photo + post cadence
   - SMS review-request automation (target: +50 reviews in 6 months to close the Slonaker's gap)
   - Frederick MD + Loudoun VA service-area pages
6. **Lead with convenience + warranty framing**, not discount language. Target homeowners 45-65, $150K+ HHI, on 0.5-2 acre lots in Berkeley/Jefferson WV, Washington County MD, and western Loudoun VA — they own the long driveways and the perimeter scope worth bundling.
7. **Seasonal launch plan:** Soft launch March 2026 (pre-paving season), full campaign April 1 with a "Spring Perimeter Refresh" promo. Re-launch in August as "Fall Hardening Package" (roof + sealcoat before winter).
8. **Track:** bundle attach rate (target ≥15% of Real Elite roofing jobs add at least driveway sealcoat in year 1), average ticket lift, and joint review velocity.
