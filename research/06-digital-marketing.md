# Digital Marketing Strategy Research: Roofing & Exterior Contractors (2025-2026)

**Prepared for:** Real Elite Contracting LLC (Martinsburg, WV - Veteran-Owned - WV/MD/VA tri-state)
**Date:** May 2026

## 1. Highest-Converting Facebook/Instagram Ad Formats

### Lead Form Ads vs Landing Page Ads

**Lead Forms (in-platform):**
- Form completion rates run 25-35% higher than landing page conversions; lead ads convert 30-40% better than redirects ([Improve & Grow](https://improveandgrow.com/contractors-and-trades/what-is-facebook-lead-form/)).
- Typical CPL: **$30-$80** on Meta ([Contractor Marketing Pros](https://contractormarketingpros.net/blog/the-cost-of-roofing-leads/)).
- WordStream 2025: Home Improvement Facebook **CVR = 5.22%**, but sector saw a 36% YoY CVR decline; cross-industry CPL averaged **$27.66** (+20.9% YoY) ([WordStream](https://www.wordstream.com/blog/facebook-ads-benchmarks-2025)).

**Landing Pages:**
- Median LP conversion = **6.6%**, top quartile >11% ([Call Digital Fire](https://www.calldigitalfire.com/post/facebook-ads-lead-generation-vs-landing-page-design-for-conversions-which-is-better-for-your-roi)).
- Sales conversion from lead-form leads runs **50-70% lower** than landing-page leads due to lower intent ([same source](https://www.calldigitalfire.com/post/facebook-ads-lead-generation-vs-landing-page-design-for-conversions-which-is-better-for-your-roi)).

### Creative Formats That Win

- **Video** is the top-performing class on Meta; 87% of video marketers say it drives leads ([ServiceTitan](https://www.servicetitan.com/blog/roofing-facebook-ads)).
- **Short-form drone timelapse** content generates 15-25% higher engagement than static images ([RoofPredict](https://roofpredict.com/blog/5-ways-drone-footage-wins-roofing-customers)).
- **Reels** require value in the first 3 seconds and silent-optimized captions ([Reach Digital Group](https://reachdigitalgroup.com/facebook-ads-for-roofing-companies/)).
- **Carousel ads** lower CPC by 20-30% and CPA by 30-50% vs single image; ROI lift up to 35% for service bundles ([AltaVista](https://www.altavistasp.com/use-facebook-carousel-ads-boost-overall-campaign-performance/)).
- A Texas roofer ran TikTok drone storm-damage Reels and saw a **40% lead lift** ([RoofPredict](https://roofpredict.com/blog/5-ways-drone-footage-wins-roofing-customers)).

### Top-Performing Ad Patterns (Meta Ad Library + agency case studies)

- Review-led: 5-star pull quote + photo + "free estimate before schedule fills" CTA ([ZeelyAI](https://zeely.ai/blog/proven-roofing-ads/)).
- Problem-first headline ("Shingles curling?") - drives down CPL ([Get Hookd](https://www.gethookd.ai/learn/roofing-facebook-ads-examples-tips/)).
- Owner-to-camera 30-60s testimonial - storm/insurance pitch.
- Average roofing-vertical CTR cited: **1.57%** ([Improve & Grow](https://improveandgrow.com/contractors-and-trades/roofing-facebook-ads/)).

### Real Elite Recommendation
Run a two-track Meta program: (1) **Reels + lead form** for top-funnel volume with veteran/storm hooks ($30-$60 CPL target), and (2) **carousel/video to LP** for mid-funnel "ready to quote" buyers in 30-mile radius of Martinsburg ($80-$120 CPL, higher close rate). Use Meta Ad Library to spy on Hagerstown/Winchester competitors weekly.

### LSA Requirements (post-Oct 20, 2025)
Google retired Google Guaranteed/Screened/License Verified, unifying under **"Google Verified" badge** ([RoofPredict](https://roofpredict.com/blog/unlock-google-local-services-ads-for-roofing-success)). Requirements: third-party background check (owners + employees), state contractor license, general liability + workers' comp insurance, active GBP, and LSA policy agreement. Approval takes **2-4 weeks**.

### Real Elite Recommendation
**Channel mix (monthly):** LSA $1,500 (anchor - lowest CPL with strongest intent), Google Search $2,000 on non-brand + branded, Meta $1,500 split lead-form/LP, Nextdoor $500 (Martinsburg/Hagerstown/Winchester zip targeting), plus continuous SEO/GBP work. **Avoid Angi/HomeAdvisor** - shared-lead economics destroy the close rate vs. owner-operated competitors. Reserve EDDM ($3-5k drops) for post-storm saturation only.

## 3. Review Generation Strategies

### Channel Performance
- **SMS:** 98% open rate, 45% response rate ([RoofPredict](https://roofpredict.com/blog/how-to-implement-systematic-reputation-management-roofing)). High-revenue roofers are **82% more likely** to use SMS as primary follow-up. 68% of homeowners prefer texting.
- **Email:** Lower open rates; works as the 3-day follow-up to non-responders.
- **In-person ask at final walk-through:** Highest intent but lowest scale; pair with QR code on invoice.
- Automated 24-48h SMS/email lifts response from **12% to 45%** ([WebFX](https://www.webfx.com/blog/home-services/roofing-marketing-guide/)).

### Timing
- Customer satisfaction curve peaks **24-48h post-completion** ([Reputigo](https://www.reputigo.com/blog/when-to-ask-for-reviews---the-best-timing-for-maximum-impact)) - solo operators in this window see 67% higher response rates.
- Some sources favor a **5-10 day** residential window ([Aginto](https://blog.aginto.com/the-right-way-to-collect-reviews-after-a-roofing-job/)).
- **Recommended:** SMS at job sign-off, second SMS at 48h, email at day 5.

### Incentives & Legal Compliance
- FTC's Consumer Reviews & Testimonials Rule went into effect **Oct 21, 2024**; FTC issued first warning letters Dec 22, 2025 ([FTC](https://www.ftc.gov/news-events/news/press-releases/2025/12/ftc-warns-10-companies-about-possible-violations-agencys-new-consumer-review-rule)). Penalty: up to **$51,744 per violation**.
- You **may** incentivize reviews IF the incentive isn't conditioned on a positive sentiment AND incentive is disclosed ([FTC Q&A](https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/consumer-reviews-testimonials-rule-questions-answers)).
- **Google's own policy bans all incentives** for Google reviews; violations cause listing suspension ([Robben Media](https://robbenmedia.com/how-to-incentivize-google-reviews-legal-strategies-that-actually-work-in-2025/)).
- **Safe approach:** No-incentive ask. Customers who got 5-star service write 5-star reviews.

### Tools
| Tool | Price/mo | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| NiceJob | $75-$125, no contract ([TrueReview](https://www.truereview.co/post/birdeye-vs-podium-vs-nicejob-vs-truereview-an-honest-comparison)) | Small contractors; fastest setup; 4.7+/5 rating |
| Podium | $399-$599, annual contract | SMS + webchat + payments unified inbox |
| Birdeye | $299-$449, annual | Multi-location, listing mgmt, 200+ directories |
| GatherUp / BrightLocal | $99-$199 | Local SEO + reviews bundle |

### Review Velocity & Local Pack Ranking
- Listings with **>=1 review/week** rank **25% higher** in the Map Pack ([Local Falcon](https://www.localfalcon.com/blog/what-is-google-review-velocity-and-why-does-it-matter-for-businesses)).
- 50 verified reviews now outrank 200 suspicious ones; a profile with 200 reviews and zero in 6 months ranks below one with 80 reviews and steady velocity ([Opositive](https://news.opositive.io/google/google-business-profile-new-local-ranking-factor/)).
- GBP signals = **32%** of local-pack weight, the single heaviest category ([Search Engine Journal](https://www.searchenginejournal.com/why-dynamic-profiles-are-the-new-local-ranking-factor/568200/)).

### Real Elite Recommendation
Adopt **NiceJob** ($75/mo, no contract) integrated with your CRM/job software. Trigger SMS the moment a job is marked "complete." Goal: **3-5 new Google reviews/week** for the first 6 months to build velocity. Photo-attached reviews count extra in dynamic GBP scoring - train crews to ask homeowners for a quick photo. No incentives.

## 4. 2025-2026 Trends Specific to Roofing

### Storm-Chasing / Response Marketing
- The 72-hour post-storm window decides 80% of insurance-claim jobs ([DG Agency](https://dgagency.co/blog/first-72-hours-after-hailstorm-roofer-playbook)).
- The first contractor to reach a homeowner wins ~**70% of jobs**; 5-min response = 2-3x conversion ([Pipeline On](https://pipelineon.com/blog/storm-damage-roofing-leads/)).
- **22% of US residential roof replacements in 2024** were storm-caused ([Knockbase](https://www.knockbase.com/blog/using-hail-trace-data-for-storm-response-sales-a-tactical-guide-for-roofing-teams)).
- Reactive-only roofers pay **$110+/lead** vs. $45-$75 for always-on competitors ([Predictive Sales AI](https://www.predictivesalesai.com/blog/p.250804000/real-time-storm-alerts-real-roofing-jobs/)).
- Playbook: within 2h of storm, raise LSA bids 50%, launch geo-targeted Google Ads on affected ZIPs, post storm photos to GBP.

### Drone Content
Vertical 9:16 before/after drone Reels are now table stakes. Add captions for sound-off scroll. Post 8-10 AM and 7-9 PM local ([RoofPredict](https://roofpredict.com/blog/5-ways-drone-footage-wins-roofing-customers)).

### AI Overviews / SGE
- Google AI Overviews appear in **68% of local business searches** ([Mr. Pipeline](https://mrpipeline.com/home-services/how-googles-ai-overviews-are-changing-local-search-and-what-home-service-businesses-need-to-do/)).
- Organic CTR dropped **61%** (1.76% -> 0.61%) on AI Overview queries ([Evergreen Media](https://www.evergreen.media/en/guide/google-ai-overviews/)).
- **22% of homeowners now go to ChatGPT first**, not Google, when searching for a contractor (Scorpion 2026 study, [Contractor Magazine](https://www.contractormag.com/management/best-practices/article/55378932/how-ai-search-is-changing-how-homeowners-find-contractors)).
- Citations are 3 of the top 5 AI-visibility factors ([Advice Local](https://www.advicelocal.com/blog/2026-local-search-ranking-factors-maps-organic-ai/)) - so build clean NAP citations on BBB, Angi profile, HomeAdvisor profile, Chamber of Commerce, veteran-business directories.
- Add **FAQ schema** + conversational service-page content so AI assistants quote your site.

### Google Business Profile (2025-2026)
- Static profiles lose: behavioral signals (post freshness, photo freshness, review velocity, booking interactions) now drive ranking ([Service Hero](https://gowithhero.com/blog/google-business-profile-2025-changes/)).
- Weekly GBP posts + monthly fresh photos = compounding ranking benefit.

### Veteran-Owned Trust Signal
Trade-press research suggests "veteran-owned" is a measurable trust lift, especially with WV/MD/VA's high military/federal population. Display the badge on website hero, GBP services description, every truck, every door-hanger, and run a dedicated "Veteran-Owned, Family-Operated" Meta ad set ([Honorable Marketing](https://www.honorable.marketing/posts/how-veteran-values-enhance-marketing-services), [Lift Digital](https://lift-digital.net/blog/7-reasons-to-hire-a-veteran-owned-digital-marketing-agency/)).

### AI-Generated Before/After Posts
Tools like Get-Ryze and Zeely now spin job photos into Meta-ready creatives. Useful for volume but **disclose if used in ads** under FTC truthfulness rules; never fake the "after."

## Strategic Summary for Real Elite

1. **Anchor on LSA + GBP** - the cheapest, highest-intent local lead source post-Verified badge.
2. **SEO is the long-game with 5x the ROAS of paid** - publish service pages for each town (Martinsburg, Hagerstown, Winchester, Charles Town, Frederick) with FAQ schema for AI Overview capture.
3. **Meta = top of funnel** - drone Reels + owner-to-camera + veteran identity, $30-80 CPL.
4. **NiceJob automation** for 3-5 reviews/week to win the Map Pack.
5. **Storm-readiness playbook pre-built** - LSA bid templates, geo-fenced Google Ad copy, EDDM mailer artwork ready to drop within 2 hours of any tri-state hail/wind event.
6. **Skip shared-lead platforms** (Angi/HomeAdvisor/Thumbtack) - economics no longer work for owner-operators.
