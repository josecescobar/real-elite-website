# Competitive Intelligence Report - Real Elite Contracting LLC
**Markets:** Eastern Panhandle WV | Frederick County MD | Loudoun County VA
**Services scope:** Roofing, siding, decks, remodeling, windows, doors, gutters
**Report date:** May 2026
**Sources reviewed:** 34

---

## Executive Summary

The three target markets are saturated with established mid-size exterior contractors (5–30+ year tenure), but the competitive set has identifiable weaknesses Real Elite can exploit:

1. **Veteran-owned positioning is partially commoditized** in NoVA (Veterans Choice Home, Valor Home) and contested in WV (Modern Renovations), but no competitor combines a Marine/Army identity with the "Military Precision" trade-execution narrative at the Eastern Panhandle level.
2. **AI-powered instant quoting is functionally absent** at the contractor-website level in all three markets. The only AI quote experience comes from third-party lead-aggregator platforms (InstantRoofer, RoofHero, Roofle) that act as middlemen — not from any local contractor's own site. **Working assumption confirmed.**
3. **Social media presence is weak across the local field** — Crafted Exterior Services (the local market leader) has only ~1.3K Facebook followers and 120 Instagram followers, leaving a wide-open paid social and organic content gap.
4. **A franchise wave has hit Martinsburg in the last 18 months** (Mighty Dog Roofing, Cenvar Roofing expansion, Titan Roofing's Martinsburg branch), signaling investor confidence in the market but also more aggressive paid acquisition.

## 3. Paid Advertising Intelligence

Google Ads Transparency Center and Facebook Ad Library both return HTTP 403 to automated fetching, so direct ad-creative scraping was not possible. Indirect findings:

- **Google Local Services Ads (LSA)** — Industry CPL in roofing $50–95; LSA convert at ~31% vs ~12% for standard PPC ([Blue Corona data](https://www.bluecorona.com/digital-marketing/paid-media/google-local-services/roofing/)). Real Elite must pass Google verification (license, insurance, background check) to compete in the LSA stack at the top of Martinsburg / Frederick / Loudoun SERPs.
- **Google Ads PPC** — Roofing standard CPC $35–95 for high-intent leads; brand search $15–25 ([Built Right Digital](https://builtrightdigital.com/roofing-google-ads-guide/)). The expected major bidders are the regional/national players: Long Roofing, Power Home Remodeling, Presidential Exteriors, Mighty Dog Roofing, Cenvar, Shumaker.
- **Facebook/Meta Ads** — Mighty Dog Roofing operates a national Facebook strategy ([11.6K page likes](https://www.facebook.com/mightydogroofing/)) and has been observed promoting a "$150 OFF repairs / $500 OFF replacements" template across franchisees. Modern Renovations (4.9K followers) and Presidential Exteriors (4.3K followers) are the most-likely organic-and-boosted local competitors on Meta.
- **Long Home / Long Roofing** — Claims [12,000+ five-star reviews and 4.7 Google rating](https://longhome.com/locations/maryland/) regionally; runs heavy PPC/social across MD/VA/DC.
- **Power Home Remodeling** — 22 states, [856 Yelp reviews at Silver Spring location](https://www.yelp.com/biz/power-home-remodeling-silver-spring); dominant on D2D and digital.

**Strategic implication:** Real Elite cannot win a head-to-head PPC bidding war against Long, Power, or Presidential on generic terms. The opening is in (a) **long-tail veteran/military intent**, (b) **AI-quote landing pages** that beat the "fill out form and wait" experience of every other competitor's PPC LP, and (c) **LSA Google-Guaranteed badge** to dominate the local pack on emergency-intent terms ("roof leak Martinsburg," "storm damage Inwood").

## 4. AI-Powered Instant Quoting in WV/MD/VA

**Working assumption: NONE of the named competitors offer AI-powered instant quoting on their own websites. Verified.**

What exists in the market:

- **Third-party aggregator platforms** (not contractors): [InstantRoofer](https://www.instantroofer.com/), [RoofHero](https://www.roofhero.com/), [Roofle](https://offers.roofle.com/), [Roofr](https://roofr.com/), [GeoQuote.ai](https://geoquote.ai/), [MyRoofer.ai](https://www.myroofer.ai/). These tools sell leads to contractors ($30+/lead via InstantRoofer) but the contractor isn't running the AI experience — the platform is. InstantRoofer lists only 7 contractors in Huntington WV and 2 in Princeton WV — **no Martinsburg / Eastern Panhandle partner contractors found in the search index**.
- **Contractor closest to self-serve:** Top Dawg Exteriors uses [BookMyEstimate](https://topdawgexteriors.bookmyestimate.com/) — calendar booking only, not pricing.
- **Modern Renovations, Crafted Exterior Services, Topper Construction, Presidential Exteriors, DuPaul, Mighty Dog Martinsburg, Titan, Cenvar, Long Roofing, Power, Veterans Choice Home, Valor Home** — all use traditional "request a free estimate" forms with human follow-up. No AI measurement, no instant pricing, no embedded chatbot quoting.

**Strategic implication — this is Real Elite's single biggest defensible advantage.**
- Embed a Roofle/Roofr/InstantRoofer-class AI quoting widget on realeliteagc.com (or equivalent), or build a custom one.
- Lead all paid ads (Google, Meta, LSA where eligible) with the headline differentiator: **"Get a roof price in 60 seconds — no salesperson, no waiting."**
- This is the first time the local market will have seen this on a contractor's own site. Expected lift in form-conversion: 6–10x based on Roofr's benchmark data of 8–10% form conversion vs ~1% on standard "contact us" pages.
- Combine with the veteran-owned brand: "Military Precision pricing — your quote in 60 seconds, your install on time, every time."

## Action Recommendations for Real Elite

1. **Own the AI-quote category locally.** Ship an instant-price tool on the site within 90 days. Trademark a phrase like "Real Elite Instant Quote" or "60-Second Estimate."
2. **Apply for Google Local Services Ads / Google Guaranteed** immediately — competitive moat in the Martinsburg local pack.
3. **Out-flank Modern Renovations on social.** They have 4.9K Facebook followers — target 10K within 12 months with veteran-identity video content (Marine/Army vs generic "veteran") + before/after job reels.
4. **Attack DuPaul Construction's expired-license vulnerability** with retargeting ads on "licensed & insured WV contractor" search terms.
5. **Use Presidential Exteriors' cleanup/warranty complaints** as the negative space — codify a written "Job Site Discipline Guarantee" (full cleanup, photo-documented, 24-hour warranty response).
6. **For Loudoun County entry**, lead with execution speed and AI quoting; veteran identity is table-stakes there (Veterans Choice Home and Valor Home already own it). For Frederick County, lead with the AI quote tool against Topper's traditional process.
7. **Monitor monthly:** Google review counts for Crafted, Top Dawg, Modern Renovations, Mighty Dog Martinsburg; Facebook Ads Library entries for the same set; new LLC registrations on WV Secretary of State (apps.sos.wv.gov).
